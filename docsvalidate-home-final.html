Features

Everything you need to ship
with confidence

Built specifically for commodity exporters — cacao, coffee, organic products — by someone who lives trade documentation every day.

⚖️

Weight & Quantity Matching

Net weight, gross weight, and lot quantities are automatically compared across all documents. Even small discrepancies are flagged before customs.

🔐

Container & Seal Verification

Container numbers and seal numbers are validated against the Bill of Lading as master — never overridden by majority voting.

🏢

Party Cross-Check

Shipper, consignee, notify party, and agent names are compared across invoice, B/L, Certificate of Origin, and all other documents.

🌿

Organic & Phyto Certificates

Deep support for COI (Kiwa BCS), Phytosanitary, TRACES NT, and certification documents specific to organic commodity exports.

📍

Port & Routing Validation

Port of loading, port of discharge, and vessel name consistency is confirmed across all shipping and commercial documents.

📄

One-Click PDF Report

Download a clean, professional validation report you can share with your team, customs broker, or buyer in seconds.
