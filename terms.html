Description of Service

DocsValidate provides an AI-powered document review platform that analyzes sets of export and trade documents — including but not limited to Bills of Lading, Commercial Invoices, Packing Lists, Phytosanitary Certificates, and Certificates of Origin — and flags potential inconsistencies across them.

The Service operates by:

  • Accepting document uploads in PDF, image, Excel, and Word formats
  • Using AI models to extract structured data from each document
  • Comparing extracted data across documents to identify potential mismatches
  • Generating a validation report with a list of observations and potential errors

The Service is a validation aid, not a definitive compliance determination. It does not constitute legal, customs, trade compliance, or financial advice.
